Understanding Air Quality

Health Effects

Air pollution affects everyone, but children and the elderly are most at risk.

Protection

Wear masks and use air purifiers when AQI levels exceed 150.

Sources

Vehicle emissions, industrial smoke, and wildfires are primary sources of PM2.5.
